Explore Filipino Dishes

Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic.

Sinigang

A comforting, sour soup typically made with pork, shrimp, or fish, flavored with tamarind.

Pancit Canton

A beloved stir-fried noodle dish loaded with veggies, meat, and shrimp. A must-have at Filipino birthdays!

Kare-Kare

A rich peanut sauce stew, often served with oxtail, tripe, and vegetables, with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side.

Tinola

A comforting chicken soup cooked with ginger, green papaya (or sayote), and malunggay (moringa leaves)

Bicol Express

A spicy coconut milk stew made with pork, shrimp paste, and lots of chili peppers.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Deep-fried mini spring rolls filled with ground pork and vegetables, often served with sweet chili sauce.

Halo-Halo

A colorful and refreshing Filipino dessert made with crushed ice, sweetened fruits, jellies, beans, leche flan, and ice cream.

Turon

A sweet snack made of sliced bananas (and sometimes jackfruit) wrapped in spring roll wrapper and fried with caramelized sugar.
