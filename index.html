Explore Filipino Dishes

Adobo

A popular Filipino dish made with meat marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic.

Sinigang

A sour soup made with tamarind, tomatoes, and various vegetables.

Pancit

A stir-fried noodle dish often served during celebrations.

Lechon

A whole roasted pig, known for its crispy skin and tender meat.

Halo-Halo

A popular Filipino dessert made with crushed ice, sweet beans, fruits, and leche flan.

Turon

A sweet snack made with bananas and jackfruit wrapped in spring roll wrappers and fried.

Bibingka

A rice cake traditionally made with rice flour and coconut milk, often topped with salted egg.

Laing

A spicy dish made with dried taro leaves cooked in coconut milk.

Longganisa

A type of Filipino sausage, often sweet and garlicky.

Empanada

A pastry filled with meat, vegetables, or cheese, often deep-fried or baked.
